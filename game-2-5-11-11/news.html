In this game all players will still be solo and cannot win together, but they will all have their own custom role that might help them win.
1:22 PM

Gaslighter

At night select any one card - choose any role in the game for them. If selected card dies during current night or next day, they will appear as a selected role. Disguised card will also appear as a selected role to informer cards. You cannot choose the same card three nights in a row. If card is not from your deck, you will also learn its real role. (edited)
1:23 PM

Bounty Hunter

At night select one card from each deck besides your deck. If any of these cards were lynched during next day - your cards will gain invincibility for the next night and day. You can't have invincibility two times in a row, but if you got your target lynched while not being able to get invincibility for next phases, it will work after this 1 night and day of you not being able to use invincibility ends. You cannot select a card in someone's deck if they have only one card left. (edited)
1:23 PM

Spy

At first night after the actions of your instant informer roles (if you have them), select one card from each deck - if a card is a villager, it becomes the Imposter. If it's a non-villager or a card that can turn into non-villager, secretly an other random card from this enemy's deck becomes the Imposter. You learn the roles of all 3 imposters. From now on, you can decide actions of these cards by yourself. Enemies will not know that their card isn't under their control and will select actions for it regularly, but they won't actually work. At day phase, if an Imposter card(s) is voting against you, it will show as you have a vote of this card, but it won't actually count towards the final results of the voting. At night enemies can decide to kill one of their own cards with their werewolf or Solo Killer. If this card is an Imposter - it dies during the night and they get a second use of their kill. Whenever an Imposter card dies, it's announced that this card was an Imposter. (edited)
1:24 PM

Sharper

At night select two of your cards, if one of these cards will be killed during current night or next day - you select if you want the other selected card to die instead of it and change places with eachother. The cards that got swapped after a successful use of your ability cannot be chosen for it anymore. Once per game at night you can activate ability that will be consumed regardless and will protect both selected cards from death this day and night. (For example I selected B2 and B6, B2 is a Loudmouth and B6 is a Jelly Wolf, my B6 Jelly Wolf is getting killed at night, but instead of it dies the Loudmouth and my Jelly Wolf is now B2) (edited)
