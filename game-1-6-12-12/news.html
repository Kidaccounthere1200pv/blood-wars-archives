Trade Cards

After the voting phase, before the night starts, there will be a trading phase.

How to offer a trade?

During the voting phase, players can select one of their own cards and select another player to offer the trade.

What happens if I'm offered a trade?

If someone has offered you a trade, you will learn this when the trading phase starts. You will be informed about: - which player is offering you a trade, and - which value range does the offered card belong to. -# Value ranges are: 0-5, 6-11, 12-20, 20+

How to accept a trade?

You can accept the trade by choosing one of your own cards from the same or higher value range. Your card and the offered card will be swapped.

What happens if I don't take place at trades?

Each time you refuse a trade OR you don't offer and aren't offered any trade, you will be punished. You will be forced to accept all trades in the next trading phase. -# If you had to reject the trade becuase you did not have any cards from that value range or higher, then you won't be punished. -# When you get rid of a card, but then you receive the same card again, your inventory will not be reset.
